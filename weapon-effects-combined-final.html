ALL WEAPON EFFECTS — FINAL

Combined reference for Code. All 4 effects animated. Drop this file in repo root.

Plasma Laser
Pointed oval core, electric side arcs, white-hot center line, trailing energy particles. Replaces normal bolts for 8s.
Energy Shield
Hex mesh bubble, gradient fill brighter at edges, rotating energy crackle arcs with bright nodes, top highlight, secondary thin ring. Absorbs one hit.
Mega Beam
3-layer beam column (ambient → red body → white core), energy rings travel upward, origin glow. Ship 1.5x. Disables normal cannon.
Phase Shift
Ship flickers 40-55% opacity, ghost echo trails behind, golden scan lines scroll over ship, diamond sparkles orbit. Asteroids pass through. 5s.
