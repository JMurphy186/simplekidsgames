Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Simple Kids Games (simplekidsgames.com) is a free, ad-free browser game site for kids. We take privacy seriously, especially because our audience includes children.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. No accounts, no emails, no names, no tracking. Period.

Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use cookies. We use your browser's localStorage to save game progress (like campaign level, high scores, and preferences). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking tools. We do not track your behavior.

Advertising

There are no ads on Simple Kids Games. We do not partner with advertisers or ad networks.

Third-Party Links

Our site contains a link to Buy Me a Coffee (buymeacoffee.com) for optional donations. This is an external site with its own privacy policy. We are not responsible for their practices.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Simple Kids Games is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. Since we collect no data at all, there is nothing to delete, export, or opt out of.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how this site works in a way that affects privacy, we will update this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our Buy Me a Coffee page.
